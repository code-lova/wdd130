Welcome to Wetwoods Whitewater Rafting

Wetwoods Whitewater Rafting is more than pushing rubber boats down a river and making up for lost time, it is also about creating a platform for doing good in our community and having an opportunity to give back. We believe in creating lasting river memories and providing a forum for others to experience the greatness of the Colorado River, the magnificence of Wetwoods Whitewater Rafting Company, along the Glenwood Springs and the Roaring Fork Valley.
